About Word Bloom

Word Bloom started in a 2019 with a vision to empower the creative spirit in children and adults. Through a series of classes, workshops and fun activites, we aim to help our students develop their creative skills and express themselves better. We believe that creativity is a skill that can be developed and nurtured. We also believe that creativity is not limited to the arts, but can be applied to all aspects of life.
